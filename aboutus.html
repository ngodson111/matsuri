About Us
Matsuri Tokyo opened on March 14, 2021 and just celebrated its one year anniversary. We are a Japanese Restaurant where you can feel the flames of authentic Japanese flavors. Matsuri stand for “festival” in Japanese and that is exactly what we hope for our customers to feel during their visit. We serve freshly prepared Japanese specialties that are a feast for the palate to allow our customers to enjoy a wonderful Japanese dining experience at our restaurant in 1 Durbar Mall, Durbar Marg, Kathmandu. We attempt to introduce you to the full range of Japanese cuisine and current trends at our restaurant. We expose the best flavor of ingredients that we hand-picked.Our service is also complemented by our authentic selection of Japanese liquors. We also offer booking for Karaoke session for groups’ upto 14-15 people at our restaurant. A special area for formal and informal gatherings is also available for reservation at Matsuri Tokyo.
